Privacy Policy for Uncrypt

Last updated: March 5, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Uncrypt. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

The Data We Collect

We only collect data from users who choose to create an account. If you play without logging in, we do not collect or store any personal information.

For logged-in users, we collect and process the following data:

  • Account information: email address, display name
  • Authentication data: password (stored securely as a hash) or Google account identifier
  • Game statistics: scores, completion times, streaks, and other gameplay metrics
  • Session data: information about your current session and login activity
  • UI preferences: your saved display preferences

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account
  • To track your game progress and statistics
  • To generate leaderboards and enable competitive features
  • To improve the game experience based on user engagement
  • To verify your identity when you log in

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in our database hosted on Replit. We retain your data for as long as you maintain an active account. If you choose to delete your account, all personal data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Third-Party Services

We use Google's authentication service to allow for Google sign-in capabilities. When you use this option, Google provides us with basic profile information according to Google's own privacy policy.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties. While our optional marketing emails may contain promotional links to third-party services, your personal information remains confidential and is never shared with these third parties.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to maintain your session and save your preferences. These are necessary for the proper functioning of the game and remembering your login status.

Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have rights including:

  • Your right of access - You can ask for copies of your personal data.
  • Your right to rectification - You can ask us to correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Your right to erasure - You can ask us to delete your personal data.
  • Your right to restrict processing - You can ask us to limit the processing of your personal data.
  • Your right to data portability - You can ask for an exportable copy of your data.

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those employees and third parties who have a business need to know.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at [your contact email].
